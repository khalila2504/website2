Your Name (Japanese: 君の名は。, Hepburn: Kimi no Na wa) is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film produced by CoMix Wave Films and distributed by Toho. It depicts a high school boy in Tokyo and a high school girl in the Japanese countryside who suddenly share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

The film was commissioned in 2014, written and directed by Makoto Shinkai. It features the voices of Ryunosuke Kamiki and Mone Kamishiraishi, with animation direction by Masashi Ando, character design by Masayoshi Tanaka, and its orchestral score and soundtrack composed by Radwimps. A light novel of the same name, also written by Shinkai, was published a month prior to the film's premiere.

Japan is one month away from the comet's first visit in a thousand years. Mitsuha, a high school girl who lives in a rural town deep in the mountains, spends her days in melancholy. To her father's campaign for mayor, the old customs of her family shrine. It's a small and narrow town, and it's just that I'm at an age where I'm more concerned about the eyes of people around me, so my longing for the city only grows stronger.

One day, I dream that I become a boy. Unfamiliar rooms, unfamiliar friends, and the cityscape of Tokyo spread out before your eyes. Mitsuha fully enjoys her long-awaited life in the city. On the other hand, Taki, a high school boy living in Tokyo, also had a strange dream. I am a high school girl in a town deep in the mountains that I have never been to. A strange recurring dream. And the memories and time that are clearly missing. The two notice.

“I/we are swapping!?”

Taki and Mitsuha accept the reality little by little, even though they are puzzled by their bodies and lives that change over and over again. Through each other's notes left behind, we sometimes fight, sometimes enjoy the other person's life, and get through the situation. However, as soon as they open up to each other, the exchange suddenly stops. As they switch places, Taki realizes that they have a special connection, and decides to go see Mitsuha.

An unexpected truth awaits them when they arrive... A meeting between two people who have never met. The gears of destiny are now starting to move.
